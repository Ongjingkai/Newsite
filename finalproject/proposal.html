Shadow Frame


What does it do?

The shadow box is an great addiction on your nightstand, the shadow box allows the user to change the background colour with a simple click of a button.
The shadow box's images are interchageable and can be customazed based on the user preference.

Features

  • -Interchangeable scenes
  • RGB color changes when clicked
