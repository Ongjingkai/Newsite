Cutting Guild

This is to show how I used my guild to correct the art to size
Please download the guild lines here if you want to repeat what I am doing. Take note that I am using Adobe Illustrator 2021.

Step 1


open guild line in Adobe Illustrator 2021


Step 2


Import the SVG you want to cutout.


Step 3


Scale the SVG file to fit the blue lines to make everything uniform. Save as DXF to maintain it's dimensions


Step 4


import the DXF files into coral draw and cut it all out. Do take note that more detailed art takes a long time to build.
For this project, use wood less or equal to 3mm to fit into the spacers.
