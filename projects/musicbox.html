Music Box


Design Process

Before I start designing anything, I always do research and write out all limitations and issue I might face.
Note down the list of requirements the item is required to help with the skatching and prototyping process.



This is my design process if you would like to follow along

  1. List down requirements, expectation, limitations of the item
  2. Research on item done by others or your past projects.
  3. Sketches and protyping(My favorate part)
  4. Test fit, final modifications, data collection and Documentation
    (Repeat step 3 if you have to)
  5. Item completed


Designing my music box

List of requirements, expectations and Limitations

Requirements Expectations Limitations
Must have a movable lid Moveable Lid Must be lasercut
Must be able to accommodate music box Contain enough space for music box Drawn and modelled in Fusion 360
Can be glued together Box to be connected via tabs no nails, hinges can be used
Must be “decorated” in some manner design can be downloaded online
Must accommodate musical hand crank Hole at the right side of the box

Research and past projects

I researched and learned how others design their music boxes and took some music box
from my collections to take some measurements that were not provided.
The infomation would be usefull when sketching later.

Measurements I need

I like to know EVERYTHING before I start any sketches so It does not affect me after I start.

Sketches and measurements

I used to use paper for sketching and designing prototypes, but I've since upgraded to an iPad
to save on paper cost. It's good to have a measuring tape when sketching to imagine how big your item would be.
The app I'm currently using is "concept".

Converting to CAD model

Start building your music box into a CAD model. If you want to download mine click here

Design and cutting

Layout your design on corel draw(the software is in the computer) and import your design into the area you want. Please ensure the design is in grayscale to reduce cutting time down.

Select a clean spot on the material before cutting

Drilling, assemble, and touchups

Measure the diameter of the music box handle(6mm)
Please place a discarded wood below the item you want to drill to not get a new hole in your table or workbench.

Finish by either hotgluing or screwing the music player into the music-box
