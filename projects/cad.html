About Fusion 360

Fusion 360 is a cloud-based 3D modeling, CAD, CAM, CAE, and PCB software platform for product design and manufacturing. In EP1000 Fusion is used to create CAD models for 3D printing and Laser cutting.

Designing a knight piece

Knowing the dimention of the part you are designing and making is the first thing you should do, provided below are the dimension of the knight piece

the one in the photo to the left. Before we step into fusion 360
This 2 shortcut keys would be your best friend! so keep that in mind.
  1. Shift+Middle mouse key to orbit
  2. Middile mouse key to move around

Step 1


Steal Select an knight image from google and download it. The one I used is on the right hand side (・ω<).

Step 2


Scatch a box with the size of the chess piece on the XZ axis

Import the image under the insert tab.

Step 3


Draw the half of the base of your chess piece

Use revolve to complete the base

Step 4


Use the shell tool to turn the base hollow.

Step 5


Draw the outline of the horse using the image inserted

Extrude the scatch by 5mm

Step 6


Cut a gap in between the ears

Finish by adding a mane to your horse
